She helped him be seen as not weak like the others thought he was. Together now they work for the queen on a secret mission to send a letter across state lines.

Randy was a kid when his older brother, the only surviving one, tried his best to sheild him from his mother. He became a drunk like his mom and forgot his brothers name but never his face.

Sheizo was wondering the city streets looting and looking for scraps before he met Bartholumule. Atan was adopted into a monestary where he found himself being treated differently to the other kids cause of his demon blood. It wasnt till he proved himself to the adults there by reading a forbidden book filled with demon magic.
